Makua Ubanagu

Makua Ubanagu is a versatile creative and communicator with experience in journalism, entrepreneurship, filmmaking, and digital media.

He is the founder of Mk Wears, a clothing brand rooted in quality and personal style.

As a journalist, he has spent over two years covering impactful stories and informing the public with clarity and purpose.

Makua also writes, directs, and produces faith-based films and dramas that inspire and reflect real-life experiences.

With strong and evolving skills in video editing and graphic design, he brings ideas to life through powerful visuals and storytelling.

His work is driven by passion, creativity, and a commitment to excellence.
