PUNCH Nigeria Ltd. – Video Production Project


As part of a project commissioned by PUNCH Nigeria Ltd., I was tasked with bringing a pre-written script to life through visual storytelling.

I developed a visually engaging production plan and took charge of directing, filming, and editing the entire project.

With support from a collaborative team, we successfully translated the script into a compelling visual narrative.

While the drone footage was outsourced, I oversaw its integration to ensure consistency with the overall visual style and tone of the production.

"What Does Education Mean?"

– De-Excel School Advertisement

I scripted, directed, and edited this compelling school advertisement, which emphasises the core educational needs of young pupils and students, specifically tailored for African and Nigerian audiences.

The ad showcases the essential standards and offerings a school must provide to foster meaningful education.

While cinematography was outsourced to a talented team member, I handled the entire production process, including scripting, directing, and video editing, ensuring a cohesive and impactful final product.

“The Father’s Will" – Early Film Project


"The Father’s Will" marks one of my earliest ventures as an aspiring filmmaker specialised in edifying and inspirational content.

I scripted, shot, directed, and edited this film, collaborating with a talented team to bring the story to life.

Reflecting on this project, my growth as a filmmaker is evident in my enhanced scriptwriting, storytelling, and ability to create compelling shot angles and polished edits, all of which continue to elevate my craft.

"Mirage" – First Film Project


In 2022, during my NYSC service as a government and literature teacher, I channeled my enthusiasm for filmmaking into my debut project, "Mirage."

Inspired by the strong bond I formed with the SS3 students, I scripted, shot, directed, and edited this film, with full support from the school management.

Beyond production, I mentored the students, teaching them the fundamentals of acting, video shooting, audio, and editing.

This project not only honed my filmmaking and editing skills but also had a profound impact on the students, fostering their creativity and technical abilities.

The Competition – Second Film Project


In late 2022, following the graduation of the SS3 students, I faced the challenge of building rapport with a new group of SS3 students.

After successfully connecting with them, I embarked on my second film project, "The Competition."

Reflecting on the lapses of my previous work, I focused on elevating my craft. Within 4 to 5 months, I significantly improved my editing, video shooting, and directing skills.

This project marked a period of substantial growth, showcasing my ability to adapt, refine my techniques, and deliver a more polished production.
